Who am I?

My name is Robine Savert & I was born in the 80's. I started coding & designing when I was about 12 years old. My first websites looked horrible, but I focused more on coding than design. When I discovered CSS, a whole new world opened.

After I learned HTML & CSS by heart, I started teaching myself Adobe Photoshop to make better designs. I mostly used GeoCities to host my websites, because back in the days, hosting was way too expensive. I bought my first domain when I was 14 & hosted my first database driven website on it (PHP).

Don't document bad code;
rewrite it!

In 2011, I obtained my SCRUM Master certificate and have been SCRUM Master for multiple projects, while also working as a Front-End Developer.

Today, I focus on clean coding and good user experience. Stylesheets need to be cascading and as neat & small as possible. I am in love with Bootstrap 3.0 & animate.css and learning more about Angular.

Before contacting me, please keep in mind that I only work in agile SCRUM teams or alone!
